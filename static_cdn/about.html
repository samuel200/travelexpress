About Us

Our Story

Travelo is proud to be one of the youngest global flight booking services out there, and thanks to our customers’ response to our offerings, we are also one of the world’s fastest-growing flight booking service. We connect our users to destinations on the map every day, with a well crafted booking system, and an unrivalled level of service from our customer care.

Travel today involves a mix of short, medium and long-haul segments, with more people travelling than ever before. With the breadth of network coverage today, virtually no destination is unreachable. This is why the commitment to service is paramount; as our guests are travelling farther and more frequently than ever before, the experience on our platform is an important part of making the journey easier.

Since our launch in 2012, Travelo has earned many customers and accolades, becoming one of an elite group of flight booking services. Travelo has won the confidence of the travelling public. We have accomplished these goals by focusing on the details – how we run the business, and how you experience our platform.
